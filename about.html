How We Started

Started with a wardrobe gap, not a business plan.

Sexy Las Trade began with a simple frustration: finding beachwear and everyday pieces that felt considered — the fit, the fabric, the styling — without paying luxury markup for the privilege. So we started sourcing and styling pieces ourselves, one bikini set and one raffia bag at a time.

What started small has grown into a full edit — bikinis, swimwear, raffia accessories, crochet, lingerie, nightwear and gym wear — all held to the same standard: it has to look like more than it costs.
